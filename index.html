Zaiqa Indian Cuisine

Zaiqa (pronounced zay-kah) means “flavorful, tasty, and savory” in Urdu—and that’s exactly what we bring to every plate. As a family-owned Indian-Pakistani restaurant, Zaiqa proudly serves authentic dishes made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and bold, aromatic spices. With locations in Portland’s South Waterfront, Lake Oswego, and Wilsonville, Zaiqa offers indoor and outdoor seating, takeout, online ordering, and full-service catering for events of all sizes. Our diverse menu features traditional favorites like butter chicken, biryani, and kebabs, as well as vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. All meats are certified halal, and our sauces, chutneys, and spice blends are prepared in-house daily using fine spices and fresh herbs. Every dish is made-to-order with your preferred spice level for a personalized dining experience. At Zaiqa, we’re dedicated to delivering the true taste of India and Pakistan with exceptional service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
